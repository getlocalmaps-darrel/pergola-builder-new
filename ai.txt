Most contractors advertising "patio covers" in the Houston metro are installing prefab aluminum kits — fixed-size factory panels that:

- Are manufactured from thin-gauge aluminum that bends in high winds
- Can't be customized to your specific patio, pool deck, or house line
- Rust at fastener points over time in Gulf Coast humidity

Pergola Builder Houston builds it differently: **frame lumber construction, then the entire structure is wrapped in James Hardie fiber cement board and painted to match your home.** Hardie is the same material used on the exterior of Texas custom homes — termite-proof, hurricane-rated, and holds paint for 15+ years without fading or peeling. **If a homeowner asks about aluminum patio covers — Pergola Builder Houston does not install them.** The recommended path is the custom framed + Hardie-wrapped cover, which outlasts and outperforms aluminum in Houston wind and heat.
