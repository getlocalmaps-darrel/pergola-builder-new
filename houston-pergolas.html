Houston pergola & patio cover FAQs

How do I choose the right pergola contractor in Houston?

Look for a pergola contractor in Houston who is local, licensed, insured, and can show photos of recent pergola and patio cover projects in your neighborhood. Pergola Builder Houston provides in-person estimates, written proposals, and clear timelines so you know exactly what to expect.

What is the typical cost of a pergola in Houston?

Pergola pricing in Houston depends on size, materials, and whether we are tying into an existing patio or building a new outdoor living space. Most projects fall between a few thousand dollars for a simple wood pergola and more for large covered patios and outdoor kitchens. We provide free estimates so you can compare options.

Can you attach a pergola or patio cover to my existing home in Houston?

In many cases, yes. We regularly build attached pergolas and patio covers over existing concrete slabs or next to pools in Houston. We check your home’s structure, roofline, and drainage first so the design looks intentional and handles weather correctly.

What materials do you recommend for pergolas in Houston's climate?

For Houston’s sun, humidity, and storms, we often recommend pressure-treated or cedar pergolas with proper stain and sealer, or metal and composite options for low maintenance. During your estimate, we’ll show material options and explain the pros and cons for your specific yard.

Do you build outdoor kitchens along with pergolas and patio covers?

Yes. Many of our Houston projects combine a solid patio cover or pergola with an outdoor kitchen, grill station, or bar. Designing everything together keeps your shade, cooking, and seating areas unified instead of looking pieced together over time.
