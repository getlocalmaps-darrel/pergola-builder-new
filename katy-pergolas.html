Katy pergola & patio cover FAQs

How do I choose the right pergola contractor in Katy?

Look for a Katy pergola contractor who understands HOA approvals, property lines, and drainage. We regularly work in Katy communities like Cinco Ranch and Grand Lakes and can help you choose a pergola or patio cover that passes review and provides real shade.

What is the typical cost of a pergola in Katy?

Simple pergolas in Katy typically start in the lower thousands and increase with size, materials, and features like lighting or outdoor kitchens. Covered patios and larger outdoor living projects cost more. We provide free estimates so you can compare options for your Katy home.

Can you attach a pergola or patio cover to my existing home in Katy?

In many Katy neighborhoods, we can build pergolas or patio covers over your existing concrete slab or next to a pool. We check how your home is built and how the water drains before recommending an attached or freestanding structure.

What materials do you recommend for pergolas in Katy's climate?

For Katy’s sun and storms, we often recommend stained cedar or pressure-treated wood with proper sealer, or metal and composite options if you prefer low maintenance. We walk you through the pros and cons for your specific backyard.

Do you build outdoor kitchens along with pergolas and patio covers?

Yes. Many Katy projects combine a pergola or patio cover with an outdoor kitchen, grilling station, or bar. Designing everything together keeps the space functional and cohesive instead of pieced together over time.
